Chez Gabriela

Born in Barcelona, Gabriela studied Elizabeth Arden's skin care technique with Elizabeth Arden protégé Maureen Russel in New York.

Gabriela continued her training with prestigious masters of skincare & beauty in Switzerland. Through years of studying and skincare practice, Gabriela developed her own version of a personalized system of beauty care treatments. Expanding her passion for skin and beauty, she partners with some of the best Swiss chemists in the business to test and perfect skincare products for some of the leading world beauty care brands.

In 1986 she opened her own private skincare studio called "Chez Gabriela Skin" in Beverly Hills that rapidly became a status symbol for beauty care in Los Angeles and has grown to reach all over US.

With special skincare techniques and hand manipulations, Gabriela developed a particular style that put her on the leading edge of beauty care today. She is widely known and referred to as the incredibly talented "skin artist" responsible for glowing skin everywhere.
